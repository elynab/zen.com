ZEN

The art of simple living

About Zen

Zen is based on teachings that are fundamentally about how humans can live in the world.

In other words, Zen is about habits, ideas and hints for living a happy life. A treasure trove, if you will, of deep yet simple life wisdom.

Zen teaching is represented by a series of four phrases. Rather than be fixated on the written or spoken word, we should encounter our essential selves as they exist in the here and now.

Try not to be swayed by the values of others, not to be troubled by unnecessary concerns, but to live an infinitely simple life, stripped of wasteful things.

This is “Zen style”.

ZEN TEACHING FOUR PARTS
  1. Try making a subtle shift in your habits
  2. Try changing your perspective
  3. Try changing how you interact with others
  4. Try shifting your affection to the present moment
PART ONE

Ways to energize your “present self”

Try making a subtle shift in your habits
  • Make time for emptiness
  • Wake up 15 minutes earlier
  • Savour the morning air
  • Discard what you don’t need
  • Organize your desk
PART TWO

Ways to inspire confidence and courage for living

Try changing your perspective
  • Discover another you
  • Don’t blame others
  • Take pleasure in your work
  • Simply immerse yourself
  • Don’t compare yourself to others
PART THREE

Ways to alleviate confusion and worry

Try changing how you interact with others
  • Serve people
  • Cultivate your sense of gratitude
  • Express your mind, but not in words
  • Focus on others’ merits
  • Don’t fixate on right and wrong
PART FOUR

Ways to make any day the best day

Try shifting your affection to the present moment
  • Be here now
  • Recognize that you are protected
  • Be positive
  • Do not covet
  • There is not just one answer
